Meal and Supplement Plan

Training Days

Cardio

45 mins empty stomach

Meal 1

  • 30ml apple cider vinegar with water
  • 200g egg whites
  • 1 scoop whey
  • 1 tbs Almond butter
  • 1g himalyan salt
  • 100g veg

Vitamins

multivitamin, 2000mg omega 3 fish oil, 1 scoop cyto greens or veggie greens, 1 500mg capsule niacin (full flush not flush free), 1 digestive enzyme, 1 GDA, 2000mg astralagus, 2 liver care capsules, 200mcg chromium, 1000mg vitamin C, 5000iu vitamin D, 400iu vitamin E, 50mg zinc, 500mg berberine

Meal 2

  • 180g chicken breast (raw Measurement) (salt and pepper) boiled
  • 100g green veg (boiled or steamed)
  • 2 tbs Olive oil or almond butter
  • 1g himalyan salt

Meal 3 (Pre-workout)

  • 180g chicken/fish/turkey (raw measurement) (salt and pepper) boiled
  • 100g green veg (boiled or steamed)
  • 200g cooked rice/200g sweet potato or potato
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1g himalyan salt

Pre-workout Drink

  • 10 grams of EAA
  • 5 grams of CITRULINE
  • 3 grams of ARGININE
  • 3 grams of L-CARNITINE
  • 2 grams of BETA-ALANINE
  • 300mg magnesium gluconate or glycinate

During Workout Drink

  • 10 grams of EAA
  • 10 grams of BCAA
  • 500ml coconut water

Post-Workout Shake

  • 30 grams of HYDROLYZED WHEY
  • 5 grams of CREATINE
  • 10 grams of GLUTAMINE

Post-workout Meal 4

  • 200g beef lean cut (raw measurement)
  • 250g rice

Meal 5

  • 10 egg whites
  • 100g green veg
  • 1g himalyan salt

Vitamins

2000mg omega 3 fish oil, ZMA dose (3 capsules), 1 digestive enzyme, 1 multivitamin, 2000mg astralagus, 500mg citrus bergamot, 2 liver care capsules or 250mg tudca, 50mg zinc, 600mg Nac, 1000mg Gaba
